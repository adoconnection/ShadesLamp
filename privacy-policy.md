# Privacy Policy

**Shades Lamp**
Last updated: April 27, 2026

## Introduction

Shades Lamp ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application for controlling LED lamps via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

### Bluetooth Data

The app connects to your LED lamp via Bluetooth Low Energy. The following data is processed locally on your device:

- **Device MAC address** — stored locally to enable automatic reconnection to your lamp
- **Device name and hardware configuration** — displayed in the app interface
- **Program data** — LED animation programs and their parameters

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

### Marketplace Data

The app downloads a public catalog of LED programs from GitHub (github.com/adoconnection/ShadesLamp). This is publicly available data and does not involve any user identification. No cookies, tokens, or personal data are sent with these requests.

### Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

- Last connected device MAC address (for auto-reconnect)
- Cached program metadata (names, descriptions, previews)

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted externally.

## Information We Do Not Collect

- We do **not** collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
- We do **not** require user accounts or registration
- We do **not** use analytics, tracking, or telemetry services
- We do **not** use advertising or ad networks
- We do **not** share any data with third parties
- We do **not** collect location data (location permission on Android is required solely for BLE scanning as per Android OS requirements)

## Data Security

All communication between the app and your LED lamp occurs over a local Bluetooth connection. No data is transmitted over the internet except for downloading the public program catalog.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
